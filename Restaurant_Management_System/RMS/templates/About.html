About Kailash Da Dhaba

Serving up a simply straightforward (and straight-up savory) menu of Indian classics like pav bhaji, aloo paratha, gobi paratha, kachori and samosa, an extensive desserts menu highlighting the best biodynamic and organic sweets from our country and world class starters from around the world, Kailash Da Dhaba is a poem to India and its culture.
